My Story

I come from a family of Civil Engineers. We immigrated in 1998 to the USA from the former Yugoslavia. When I started college, I originally wanted to go into Medicine. That was the plan. 18 Years and 2 kids later, somewhere along the way plans got lost and got unaccomplished. After working through college in the credit card industry and staying there for the last 18 years, I made the choice to switch careers and seek different opportunities.
